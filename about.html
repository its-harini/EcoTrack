About EcoTrack AI

Smart tracking for a sustainable tomorrow 🌎

Our Mission

EcoTrack AI is a smart carbon footprint tracking platform designed to empower individuals to live sustainably. We believe that small, informed actions — when taken collectively — can create a massive positive impact on our planet.

How It Works

EcoTrack AI uses intelligent algorithms to:

  • 📊 Track your daily, weekly, monthly, or annual emissions.
  • 🤖 Analyze patterns using AI-driven calculations.
  • 🌱 Suggest personalized tips for greener living.

Why AI?

Our AI model continuously learns from user patterns to deliver unique, personalized suggestions that fit your lifestyle. Whether you’re a student or a professional, EcoTrack AI helps you make greener choices every day.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make sustainability accessible, measurable, and achievable for everyone. EcoTrack AI combines technology with environmental awareness to create a community where every user becomes a planet protector.

🌍 “When we care together, the planet heals and we breathe easier.”

Join us in building a cleaner, smarter, and sustainable future — one footprint at a time.
